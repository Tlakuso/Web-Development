SPIRIT OF PRAISE

ABOUT

Spirit of Praise is a South African award-winning Gospel music group, which consists of several talented gospel artists and singers. As a group, their music project intense worship and praise with a sounding soloist rhymes. The Spirit Of Praise music group was formed to gather, enlighten, encourage and inspire the Christian community towards a greater relationship and bonding with the Lord. The South African award-winning music band has consistently released various projects with tremendous traditional gospel music styles and powerful contents. There Albums currently trends on all online music platforms, and there performance has greatly enriched several lives in South Africa and beyond.

The Spirit Of Praise Family has released a total of 8 (Volume One to Eight) successful albums over a span of 15 years. Now that’s dedication! Volume 1 & 2 was recorded in 2008 at the “Pretoria State Theatre” with artist; Keke, Andile B, Avis, Shana, Twasa, Tshepiso, Mari Micheal and Solly Mahlangu. Volume 3 was recorded in 2010 at the “Lyric Theatre” with artists; Benjamin Dube, Solly Mahlangu, Mari Micheal, Zaza, Tshepiso, Andile B & Kgotso. Volume 4 was recorded in 2012 at the big top Arena with artist; Benjamin Dube, Kgotso, Zaza, Tshepiso, Omega. Volume 5 was recorded in 2014 at the “Big Top Arena” with artist; Benjamin Dube, Zaza, Tshepiso, Omega, Neyi, Joey Mofoleng,Vicky Vilakazi, Sello Malete and The Dube Brothers. Volume 6 was recorded in 2016 at the “Big Top Arena” with artist; Benjamin Dube, Neyi Zimu , Omega , Tshepiso, Women In Praise, Dube Brothers, Dumi Mkokstad, Winnie Mashaba, Rofhiwa, Dr. Tumi, Tshepang & Nothando. Volume 7 was recorded in 2018 at the “Big Top Arena” with artist; Benjamin Dube, Neyi Zimu, Omega, Dube Brothers, Women in Praise, Tshepang Mphuthi, Dumi Mkokstad, Nothando, Thinah Zungu , Sipho Ngwenya, Mmatema, Ayanda Ntanzi , Collen Maluleke, Bongi Damans and Takie Ndou. Spirit Of Praise has scooped up 7 Crown Awards and 3 SAMA awards throughout the years. Volume 8 also recorded at “Big Top Arena” in 2022 during COVID with Artist : Benjamin Dube, Omega, Dube Brothers, Women in Praise, Mmatema, Ayanda Ntanzi , Collen Maluleke, Takie Ndou, Joey Mofoleng, Mpumi Mtweni Phindi P and Canaan Nyathi. Spirit of Praise 8 has been at #1 on The Gospel Digital charts since its release.
